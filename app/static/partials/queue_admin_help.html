Queue Admin…

This is the Queue Administrator page where administrators can administer their queues:

First Person: This will remove the person at the front of the line.

Demote: click on a person in the queue and click this button to move them back one position in the queue.

Add: this button is used to manually add anonymous persons to the back of the line.

Remove: click on a person in the queue list to select them, then click this button to remove them from the queue.

View Details: click on a person in the queue list to select them, then click this button to see any avilable details about that person.

Settings: this button will take you to a page where you can see and adjust settings for the queue you are currently viewing.

Close Queue: clicking this button blocks entrance to the queue. If you are an administrator or manager of a queue, you will maintain the ability to add and remove people form the queue.
