Create a Queue…

This is where you create a queue. There are a number of fields to fill in for creating a queue. Only the name is required. All others are optional.

Name: The name that will be used to identify your queue

Location: The physical location in which your services are being rendered

Keywords: A list of words that can be used to match your queue to search queries

Max Queue Size: The maximum number of people allowed in your queue at a given time

Minimum Time Before Re-join Queue: A time restraint for people that use your queue. When a person leaves the queue, they will not be able to reenter themselves until this amount of time has passed.

Website: If you have a website you would like display with your queue, enter it here.

Organization: The name of your orgranization

Queue Entry Disclaimer: A disclaimer that will be displayed on the page that clients use to join your queue

Prompt For Information: If you would like a particular piece of information from each of the people joining your queue, you can enter a question here that clients will answer as they joing your queue

Administrators: A list of lineup users that will be allowed to make administrative changes to this queue

Managers: A list of lineup users that will have heightened access to the queue, but will not be able to change core settings
